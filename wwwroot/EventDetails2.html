Karaoke Night: Sing Your Lungs Out

Date: Wednesday, April 24
Time: 06:00 PM
Location: CUT FNB Rugby Stadium

Get ready to unleash your inner superstar at our Karaoke Night: Sing Your Lungs Out event!

Join us on Wednesday, April 24th, at 6:00 PM at the CUT FNB Rugby Stadium for a night filled with music, laughter, and unforgettable performances.

Whether you're a seasoned karaoke pro or a first-time singer, this event is the perfect opportunity to showcase your vocal talents and have a blast with friends.

Our extensive song library features a wide range of genres and hits, so there's something for everyone to sing along to. From classic rock anthems to the latest pop chart-toppers, the stage is yours to own!

Don't miss out on the chance to be the star of the show and make memories that will last a lifetime. Grab your friends, warm up those vocal cords, and get ready to sing your heart out!
